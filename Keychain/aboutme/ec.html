FutureTeller

Project by Deven Maheshwari



This project is meant to provide the user with information about their zodiac sign and horoscope.
Enter the zodiac page where you can put in your date of birth and find out which sign you are
and either find your horoscope from there or navigate to the horoscope page. The constellation
page will test your knowledge of astronomy, where you can learn about constellations and asterisms and
take a very fun and informative quiz.

Sources:
To construct the HTML, I used a tool called BlueGriffon. For the horoscopes, I used a website
called horoscope.com where you can go to find a more in-depth horoscope.
