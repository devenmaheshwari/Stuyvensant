Welcome to KnowYourself

Project by Matt Melucci and Deven Maheshwari

Project Description:

This is our submission for the interactive website as requested by Mr. Brooks of Stuyvesant High School in New York City. Similar to a well-known entity known as Buzzfeed, this website will give you insights into your very own self through intense and challenging personality quizzes. If you dare to venture into the unknown, click the "Quizzes" tab above. If you are feeling particularly adventurous, click the "Storyteller" tab and explore your inner writer. Enjoy this website and Stephen A. Smith!
